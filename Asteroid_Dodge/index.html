Asteroid Dodge

  • High Concept: A game where you attempt to dodge the Asteroids for as long as you can as they continue to speed up.
  • Theme: Asteroids
  • Mandated Variety: Input involves moving left and right. The asteroids are all placed randomly and fall at semi random speeds.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should feel challenged to get to a new time every time they play the game, with the challenge increasing the further they go.
  • Gameplay: The player can move left and right while trying to dodge as many asteroids that have semi- random speeds and positions as the player is building up speed. In its current state, there isn't a lot else to it, but the gameplay could be expanded to include powerups, enemies, and more varied styles of gameplay.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype was designed to test the action of dodging asteroids.
  • Features: Since this is the first phaser game that I have created, I wasn't able to go into a lot of unique territories with my game design.
  • Setting/Premise: The game has no story or setting to speak of, but is set in outer space, where your ship is frantically trying to dodge asteroids.
  • Story: The game has no relevant story, and revolves entirely around dodging asteroids that come towards you.
  • Target Audience: This game is simple enough for anyone to play, so any demographic could get into it.
  • Play Time: It takes about 1-4 minutes to play a game.
  • Strategies: This is not a very strategic game, but the primary strategy revolves around dodging asteroids.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Spaceship: Spaceship
    • Asteroid: Asteroid
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game resembles quite a few games which revolve around the player being located at the bottom of the screen and dodging that which comes at them.
